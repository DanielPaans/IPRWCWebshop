About Rocking man

Who are we

We at Rockingman are motivated by the love for guitars. We want to serve and serve all guitarists. All Rockingman employees are guitarists, guitar enthusiasts and will not compromise when it comes to our instruments and equipment. We only carry stuff that we know you'll love for years to come. Rockingman is more than your average store. We offer a pleasant atmosphere, great service and personal assistance from people who are guitarists themselves.

Products

The products we sell at the moment are guitars (electric, semi and acoustic), amplifiers, picks, snares and other related guitar products. All our instruments are delivered ready to play!
